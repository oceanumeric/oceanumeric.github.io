## Act 1, Scene 1

_Vienna. Inside the Duke Vincentio's palace. Enter the DUKE, ESCALUS and LOARDS_. <br>

<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">DUKE</div>
    <div class="text">
    Escalus!
    </div>
</div>

<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">ESCALUS</div>
    <div class="text">
    My lord?
    </div>
</div>

<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">DUKE</div>
    <div class="text"> 
    The  properties of government are waiting to unfold<br>
    Would seem I shall deliver them in speech and discourse;<br>
    Since I (am put to) know that your own science<br>
    Exceeds (in that) the lists of all my advice<br>
    My strength can be then given to you.<br> 
    Now, there is no more remains, except that to your sufficiency<br>
    Just as your worth is able to do, and then let them work.<br>
    The nature of our people, Our city's institutions, and the terms<br>
    For all common justice, you're as pregnant as artful arms<br>
    Your practices could have only enriched anyone we remember. <br>
    There's our commission, from which we don't want you to waver.<br> 
    <em>[He hands it to Escalus, To a lord:]</em> 
    Call up Angelo; ask him to come to me. <br>
    <em>[Exit a lord, To Escalus:]</em> <br>
    What kind of figure do you think Angelo will bear?<br>
    He has been chosen to fill in my duty for my absence affair.<br>
    Lent him our terror, dressed him with our love,<br>
    And given his deputation of all organs of our own power.<br>
    What think you of it? <br>
    </div>
</div>


<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">ESCALUS</div>
    <div class="text"> 
    If any in Vienna be of worth<br>
    To undergo such ample grace and honour<br>
    It is Lord Angelo. 
    </div>
</div>

_Enter ANGELO_ <br>

<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">DUKE</div>
    <div class="text"> 
    Look, here he comes. <br>
    </div>
</div>

<div class='a'>
    <div class="name">ANGELO</div>
    <div class="text"> 
    Always obedient to your Grace's will <br>
    I come to know your pleasure. <br>
    </div>
</div>
