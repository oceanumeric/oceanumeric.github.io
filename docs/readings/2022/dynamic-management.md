# Organizational Innovation: A Dynamic View

!!! quote
    Man cannot grasp the meaning of information about his environment without
    some frame of value judgement. 


It is widely observed that the society we live in has been gradually 
turning into a "knowledge society". The ever increasing importance of knowledge
in contemporary society calls for a shift in our thinking concerning innovation
in large business organizations - organizational innovation. 

The theory of organization has long been dominated by a paradigm that
conceptualizes the organization as a system that 'processes' information or
'solves' problems. Yet a critical problem with this paradigm follows from its
passive and static view of the organization. However, any organization that
_dynamically_ deals with a changing environment ought not only to process
information efficiently but also create information and knowledge. 

So, the key question is: how could an organization create information and knowledge? 

## Knowledge and Information

by the very flow of information, anchored on the commitment and beliefs of
