# Legacies In Leadership

Henry Kissinger said we were now living in a totally new era after the Ukraine war.
This pushes me to think more about leadership. The grand challenges our generation
will face are piling up. It seems to me that there is no solution for all unless
each of us will work together and figure out the way. 

When I talked to people in my place, I do sense that the empowerment of 
individuals and diffusion of power among states and from states to informal 
networks. With the trend of the empowerment of individuals, we see our 
society becomes more dynamic and diverse. However, a considerable amount of 
friction grows together with this trend. One could either avoid frictions 
or cohere those frictions. 

In this process, the leadership is needed. It depends upon the mood of the
people at a given time, the situation we are in, and the set of polices that
leaders must achieve to advance and promote the interests of people. However,
there is no Jupiter around from the older generation. 

In our time, could anyone have a leadership that inspires a younger generation 
no longer deprived, widely traveled, comfortable in their homes, partying at
Saturday night, knowledgeable of outside world but will still fire in their 
hearts and bellies to achieve?
