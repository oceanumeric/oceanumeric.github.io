    Co-citations of Top Management Journals¶

    Introduction¶

    In this post, I will try to build up a co-citation network for the following top 10 management journals with searching keyword innovation in the title and abstract.

    • Administrative Science Quarterly
    • Academy of Management Journal
    • Academy of Management Review
    • Strategic Management Journal
    • Organization Science
    • Management Science
    • Research Policy
    • Journal of Financial Economics
    • Technovation
    • Technological Forecasting and Social Change

clean_strings <- function(strings) {
    str_len = nchar(strings)
    first_char <- substr(strings, 1, 1)
    if(identical(first_char, ' ')) {
        return(substr(strings, 2, str_len))
    } else {
        return(strings)
    }
}
