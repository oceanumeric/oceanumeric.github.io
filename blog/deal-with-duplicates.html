Deal With Duplicates in R

Inspecting big data could be challenge in terms of finding duplicates and dropping them.

r, python, data-science, big-data

Published

05 March 2023

Finding unique and duplicate values in a large dataset happens very often for people who are dealing with big data.

Although the unique() function in R could give us the unique values in any dataset. However, sometimes, we want to inspect those duplicates with our eyes. How could we do this?

# inspect duplicates
n_occur <- data.table(table(ipc1$patentNumber))

n_occur %>%
    .[N > 1] %>%
    head()
